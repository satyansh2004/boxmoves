BoxMoves – A Simple HTML5 Puzzle Game

BoxMoves is a fast and fun browser-based puzzle game where you move a box using quick reflexes and smart positioning. The goal is to score as high as possible within the selected time limit.

How to Play

  • Choose speed and time limit
  • Click Play to start
  • Move the box to score points
